## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. How do I register if I received an invitation?
Click "Invited Guest" on the homepage, enter your email address, and we'll send you a personalized registration link. Click the link in your email to complete your registration. The link is valid for 24 hours.

### 2. I didn't receive my invitation email
First check your spam/junk folder. If you still can't find it, return to the homepage and request a new link. Enter your email address and we'll send a fresh invitation within minutes.

### 3. What if I'm not on the guest list?
You can apply to attend by clicking "Apply to Attend" on the homepage. Fill out the application form with your details, and our team will review your request. You'll receive an email notification once approved.

### 4. How long does the application review take?
Applications are typically reviewed within 2-3 business days. Once approved, you'll receive an invitation link via email to complete your registration.

### 5. What information do I need to register?
You'll need your full name, email address, phone number, company name, and position. You can also provide dietary requirements and seating preferences.

### 6. Can I bring a guest or partner?
Invited guests can now add a free partner during registration! Simply select the option to bring a partner and enter their details. Paired ticket holders also register with their partner. Both you and your partner will receive separate tickets and access to the Gala App.

### 7. What payment methods are accepted?
We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) via Stripe, and bank transfers. Invited guests attend free of charge.

### 8. When will I receive my ticket?
Your e-ticket with QR code is sent immediately after successful payment. For bank transfers, tickets are sent within 24-48 hours after payment verification.

### 9. What is the Gala App?
The Gala App is our mobile-friendly web app where you can view your QR ticket (works offline!), check your table assignment, and update your profile. Your ticket email includes a login code (CEOG-XXXXXX) - just enter it to access the app. No password needed!

### 10. How do I access my ticket on my phone?
Open the Gala App link from your ticket email and enter your 6-character login code (e.g., CEOG-A1B2C3). Your QR ticket will be saved for offline use, so it works even without internet at the event!
